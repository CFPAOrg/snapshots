Leather
Leather provides a tough protective layer that absorbs half the damage until it'll break eventually.\n\nCannot be worn together with {link:Rabbit Hide|upgrades:rabbithide}.\n\n§7-§1 Misc. upgrade\n§7-§1 20 uses\n§7-§1 50%% less damage taken\n§7-§1 incompatible with {link:Rabbit Hide|upgrades:rabbithide}
Rabbit Hide
As with {link:Leather|upgrades:leather}, Rabbit Hide provides a weaker protective layer that absorbs 1/8th the damage, but what it lacks on protective abilities, it makes up for it by giving the wearer a speed boost.\n\nCannot be worn together with Leather.\n\n§7-§1 Misc. upgrade\n§7-§1 20 uses\n§7-§1 12.5%% less damage taken\n§7-§1 25%% speed boost\n§7-§1 incompatible with {link:Leather|upgrades:leather}
