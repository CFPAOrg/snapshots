Pushing
Blesses the bow with an ability to repel nearby entities while charging. 
Unsheathing
When taking damage (there must be an attacker), the sword will automatically switch place with whatever the user is currently holding. Does not work if the sword is not in the main hotbar.
Ancient Sword Mastery
An ancient enchantment that somewhat make the user proficient at their sword. At level 3, it also makes damaging-enchantments 1.5x more effective. May not work on other mods' enchantments.  
True Strike
Allows the user to penetrate through the evasion enchantment by 75% and prevents the user from obtaining the curse of inaccuracy enchantment. 
Pandora's Curse
